Common Copier Tips

Check the animated guide on your copier's touch screen. Open the indicated cover and gently remove paper. Check all trays. If the message won't clear, power off for 30 seconds and restart. Still stuck? Call us.

"Toner Low" means you can keep printing for a while. Email us and we'll deliver a replacement. "Toner Empty" means call us — we'll prioritize delivery. Never use third-party toner.

Streaks or lines usually mean the drum needs attention — call us. Faded prints may mean low toner. Colors off? Try running color calibration from Settings. If that doesn't fix it, call us.

Press the User Tools / Counter button on the control panel. Note the B&W and Color counters. Email the numbers to info@bayareaofficesystems.com with your company name.

Windows: Settings > Printers & Scanners > Add a Printer. Mac: System Preferences > Printers & Scanners > "+". If the copier doesn't appear, restart both devices and try again. Still stuck? Call us.

This is configured during installation. If you need changes (new email, new folder), call us and we can update it remotely or on your next service visit.

Paper jam you can't clear: call. Toner low: email, you can keep printing. Toner empty: call. Streaks or smudges: call. Error code: note the code, call. Machine won't turn on: check power cable first, then call.
